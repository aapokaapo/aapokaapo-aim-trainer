How to Participate

  1. Register to Leaderboard
  2. Bookmark the official game mode: AapoKaapo Aim Trainer V4 on Halo Waypoint.
  3. Launch Halo Infinite and navigate to the Custom Games menu.
  4. Select Create Custom Game.
  5. Select your bookmarked AapoKaapo Aim Trainer V4 as the gamemode.
  6. Select Live Fire - Ranked as the map.
  7. Start the game and race to 100 kills!
✅ Automatic Tracking: As long as you have registered your Gamertag using the button above, your times will be automatically fetched and included in this leaderboard. You do not need to manually upload anything!
⚠️ Share Match History: Set Match History Privacy under Game Settings > Accesibility to Share or your time won't appear on the leaderboard.
